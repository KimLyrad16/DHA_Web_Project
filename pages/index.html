About Our Company

DHA Metal Fabrication Services provides custom stainless steel solutions for commercial kitchens. From small food stalls to full-scale restaurant kitchens, we design, fabricate, and install equipment that matches your actual workflow.

We handle on-site measurement, design proposals, and installation. Our goal is to deliver durable, easy-to-clean, and efficient stainless kitchen equipment that helps your team work faster and safer.

Why stainless?

Stainless steel is hygienic, heat-resistant, and easy to sanitize, making it ideal for food preparation areas and commercial kitchens.
